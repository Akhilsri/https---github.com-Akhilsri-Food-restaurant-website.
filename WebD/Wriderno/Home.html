Frequently Asked Questions
  • Q1. How does E-Ink Technology Work ?

    • E Ink technology operates by controlling charged particles in tiny microcapsules within a display. Applying electrical charges to these particles causes them to move, creating a visual pattern that represents text and images. E Ink displays are reflective, like traditional paper, using ambient light for visibility. They are known for their exceptional readability in various lighting conditions and are highly energy-efficient, requiring power only when the content changes. This technology's paper-like appearance, low power consumption, and persistent display make it ideal for e-readers and other devices where readability and long battery life are essential.

  • Q2. How does an E Ink tablet differ from a regular tablet?

    • E Ink tablets use E Ink displays, which are highly readable in bright sunlight, consume very little power, and resemble printed text on paper. Regular tablets typically have backlit LCD or OLED displays and are designed for various multimedia tasks.
